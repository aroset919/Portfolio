Top 5 Movie Picks as of 2024

Elementals

Probably one of the best depictions of romance I have seen in a long while. The characters are unique and relatable; the narrative is well paced; and the story flows seamlessly.

Avengers: Endgame

This marks the last of Marvel's great movies outside of the Spiderman franchise. Endgame brought an end to an era, and was a perfect culmanation of a story that had been built over multiple intertwining plotlines.

One Piece Film: Red

I am a huge One Piece fan and this movie was my gateway for a lot of people into the series. Uta was a fantastic addition to the story and the music is still saved on my Spotify playlist.

The Super Mario Movie

This movie was often criticized for lacking a plot, but I adore it becuase of it's commitment to the core of what the Mario franchise is: fun. The Mario games never emphasized story, and yet remained a huge hit. I think the movie reflects that, an experience the viewer is meant to have fun enjoying.

Howl's Moving Castle

Studio Ghibli has been, and will always be, one of the my favorite publishing houses. I credit them and this movie in particular for sparking my interest in anime. Howl's moving castle will always be a childhood favorite of mine.
